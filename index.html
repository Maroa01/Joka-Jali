About Us

Our Story

Joka Jali is a family-owned farm in Kajiado, Kenya, dedicated to cultivating premium dragon fruit. What started as a small venture has grown into a thriving farm with over 2,000 plants, driven by our passion for quality, sustainability, and innovation.

Our Journey

From planting our first dragon fruit to developing freeze-dried powders and snacks, our mission has always been to bring the best of nature to health-conscious consumers. As we expand into Migori, Eldoret, and Kilifi, we remain committed to excellence in farming and processing.

Our Dream

We envision Joka Jali as a leading brand in luxury health foods, offering fresh and freeze-dried dragon fruit directly to consumers. Our goal is to redefine how people experience this superfruit while fostering sustainable growth for our farms and community.

Joka Jali—where nature meets luxury.
